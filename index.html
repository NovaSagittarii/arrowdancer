arrowdancer v2 (with gemini&capricorn support)

NOTE: most bugs should be fixed now; may miss solutions starting with fast tech though

This tool will find a dance sequence given the steps you give it. (Note: this is intended to support divide&conquer skill).
Steps are in order of left to right, empty steps are ignored.
Click a corner/edge box of the square to set its state.
Scroll to bottom and click solve when you are done.
Numbers at the bottom indicate how many attacks are done during that state.
Letters at the bottom (with those numbers) are entry points into the cycle.
